About Limited Defense

Limited Defense is an engaging tower defense strategy game that challenges players to defend their base against relentless waves of enemies. What sets this game apart is the limited resources you have at your disposal, forcing you to make strategic decisions about tower placement and upgrades.

Each level presents unique challenges and enemy types, requiring you to adapt your strategy and think critically about resource management. Can you hold the line with your limited defenses?

The game combines classic tower defense mechanics with a unique resource limitation system, creating a challenging and addictive gameplay experience that will test your strategic thinking and adaptability.

Strategic Tower Placement

Position your defensive towers strategically to maximize their effectiveness against incoming enemy waves. Create choke points and defensive lines to control the battlefield.

Resource Management

Manage your limited resources carefully to build, upgrade, and repair your defenses as the battle intensifies. Every decision counts when your resources are scarce!

Progressive Difficulty

Face increasingly challenging waves of enemies that will test your strategic thinking and adaptability. Can you survive all waves with your limited defenses?

Tips & Strategies

Early Game Strategy

Focus on building basic towers at key choke points to slow down the initial waves. Don't spend all your resources immediately - save some for emergency situations and unexpected enemy paths.

Tower Synergies

Combine different tower types to create powerful synergies. For example, pair slowing towers with high-damage towers to maximize enemy exposure to damage and create effective defensive zones.

Upgrade Strategy

It's often better to have a few highly upgraded towers than many basic ones. Focus your resources on strategic upgrades rather than building too many towers to maximize your defensive potential.

Advanced Strategies

  • Study enemy patterns and plan your defenses accordingly. Different enemies may require different tower combinations.
  • Don't neglect area-of-effect towers when dealing with swarms of weaker enemies.
  • Position your most powerful towers at intersections where they can target enemies from multiple paths.
  • Sometimes selling and repositioning towers is necessary as the battle evolves.
  • Pay attention to the upcoming wave information to prepare your defenses for specific enemy types.
